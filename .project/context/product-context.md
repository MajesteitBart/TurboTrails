# Product Context

## Audience

Children playing on laptop, desktop, tablet, or touch laptop in landscape orientation.

## Core Loop

1. Choose world.
2. Choose level.
3. Choose vehicle.
4. Race to the finish.
5. Collect coins and chests.
6. Perform stunts for EXP and bonus coins.
7. Use checkpoints and restart quickly after crashes.
8. Earn stars and unlock new vehicles, levels, and worlds.

## Required Wishes

- Crossen, coins, and stunts.
- Bigger 2.0 feel: more worlds, higher jumps, more vehicles.
- Vehicles: bikes, cars, stunt car, mini bike, long bike, Mars truck later.
- Worlds: forest, warehouse, mine, then jungle, zoo, Arctic, moon, Mars, desert canyon, obstacle zone.
- High ramps, half-loops, loopings, wall-rides, launch barrels, rolling barrels, low gravity.
- Rider choices for Levi, Noah, and Player.

## Product Boundaries

No backend, ads, microtransactions, lootboxes, copied assets, real brands, or realistic violence.
